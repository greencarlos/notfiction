124 Nagarjuna, Madhyamika Shastra.

“What is immortal and what is mortal are harmoniously blended, for they are not one, nor are they separate” (Ashvaghosha).

“This view,” writes Dr. Coomaraswamy, citing these texts, “is expressed with dramatic force in the aphorism, Yas klesas so bodhi, yas samsāras tat nirvānam, ‘That which is sin is also Wisdom, the realm of Becoming is also Nirvana’ “ (Ananda K. Coomaraswamy, Buddha and the Gospel of Buddhism; New York: G. P. Putnam’s Sons, 1916, p. 245).
