27 Katha Upanishad, 3-14. (Unless otherwise noted, my quotations of the Upanishads will be taken from Robert Ernest Hume, The Thirteen Principal Upanishads, translated from the Sanskrit, Oxford University Press, 1931.)

The Upanishads are a class of Hindu treatise on the nature of man and the universe, forming a late part of the orthodox tradition of speculation. The earliest date from about the eighth century B.C.
