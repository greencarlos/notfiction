Joseph Campbell was born in New York City in 1904 and educated in America, France and Germany. He is the author of the four-volume study of world mythologies collectively tided The Masks of God, among other works. He has also edited The Portable Jung and The Portable Arabian Nights. He lives in Hawaii.
