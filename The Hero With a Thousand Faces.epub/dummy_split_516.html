22 Words written over the Gate of Hell:

Per me si va nella città dolente, Per me si va nell’ eterno dolore, Per me si va tra la Perduta Gente’.—Dante, “Inferno,” III, 1-3.

The translation is by Charles Eliot Norton, The Divine Comedy of Dante Alighieri (Boston and New York: Houghton Mifflin Company, 1902); this and the following quotations, by permission of the publishers.
